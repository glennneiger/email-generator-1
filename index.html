Tour Policies & Suggestions

Duration

Tours last about two hours.

Weather / Refunds / Exchanges

With rare exceptions, all tours proceed rain or shine, and all sales are final. There are no refunds, cancellations, or exchanges.

Travel

If you are using public transportation to meet your tour leader, we strongly recommend checking your route using the MTA Weekender before you leave the house for updated transit schedules, in case of service changes or delays. Please strive to arrive 10 minutes early for the start of the tour. Refunds will not be given if you arrive late to the tour and miss the group.

Questions

Please email tours@hsnyc.org with any questions about your tour and visit www.hsnyc.org to learn more about our upcoming tours and programs, become a member, and more. Historical Society members receive the seasonal tour schedule first, giving them the opportunity to purchase tour tickets early before they sell out.
